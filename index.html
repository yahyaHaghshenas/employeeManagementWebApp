Welcome to FlexIs, a comprehensive solution for managing flexible working arrangements in the workplace. With the growing demand for remote and hybrid work, it is more important than ever for organizations to have a system in place to support the management of flexible working arrangements.

FlexIs was designed to streamline the process of recording and managing employee requests for flexible working arrangements, while also providing real-time analytics and insights to supervisors and HR administrators. Whether you are an employee looking to request flexible working arrangements, a supervisor tasked with reviewing and managing employee schedules, or an HR administrator tasked with tracking compliance and performance, FlexIs has everything you need in one convenient platform.

With FlexIS, you can:
  • Submit requests for flexible working arrangements, and update your daily schedule as needed
  • Monitor and review employee requests, schedules, and performance data
  • Gain real-time insights and analytics on employee performance, productivity, and compliance
